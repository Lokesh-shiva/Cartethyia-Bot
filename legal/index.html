See it in action

Rich embed cards, straight in Discord

Every command returns a beautifully rendered card — no external apps, no browser tabs.

/boss
Boss Battles
Challenge one of 9 world-tier bosses in turn-based combat. Watch the vibration bar shatter as you land crits. Defeat them before they enrage at 40% HP — when they hit back harder and shatter recovery drops to 60%.
/profile
Character Profile
Your full character sheet at a glance — level, element, stats, equipped weapon with its Weapon Bond lore, Resonance Aura charges, and your recent party in the sidebar. Every stat scales with gear, echoes, and set bonuses.
/echo
Echo Cards
Each echo is a canvas card with rarity, element, main stat, and up to four substats that scale 1.5× by Lv 25. Hunt field bosses and dungeons to collect them, then build set bonuses for powerful passive effects.
/weapons · /equip
Weapon Arsenal
Browse your full weapon collection with a select menu — each entry shows rarity, ATK, level, and its Weapon Bond flavor text. Equip from a side-by-side comparison card before you commit. Awakened weapons display their AI-forged name and unique lore.
/stats
Combat Stats
See every final number the game actually uses — HP, ATK, DEF, Crit, Elem DMG, and a full breakdown of every bonus source: innate element bonuses, echo substats, set effects, weapon passive, and your unique evolved ability effects.

Lv 50 · /evolve

Ability Evolution

Complete a three-part quest — dungeon clears, a WL5+ boss kill, and a material cost — to permanently evolve your ability. The AI rewrites its name, lore, and adds a fourth effect using your full combat history, gear, and personality bonds.

Lv 60 · /awaken

Ego Weapon Awakening

Requires an evolved ability and an equipped weapon. Stats are amplified by rarity (up to ×1.25), the passive is enhanced, and the AI adds a new effect plus a unique awakened name, lore, and art description — all permanent.

Quick reference

Key commands

All interactions happen through slash commands — no prefixes, no memorisation required. Type / to get started.

/profileView your character stats, element, and progression
/bossChallenge the world-level boss for echoes and keys
/dungeonRun an aura-gated dungeon for drops and EXP
/wishPull on the Resonance gacha banner with Fracture Keys
/echoBrowse and equip your echo collection
/echo-upgradeLevel up echoes with +1, +5, or Auto mode
/weaponInspect your equipped weapon card with hidden substats
/weapon-upgradeLevel up weapons with +1, +10, or Auto mode
/forgeCraft weapons using Forging Ores from dungeons
/duelChallenge another player — public or private match
